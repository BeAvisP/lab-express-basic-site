Abilities

Black Arrow

45 yrd range. Curses an enemy, inflicting Shadow damage every 3 sec for 30 sec. If the target dies while afflicted by Black Arrow, a Skeleton will be summoned from the target's corpse. 2 sec cast. Cannot be interrupted.

Fade

Turns the caster invisible and increases its movement speed by 50% for 3 sec.

Multi-Shot

30 yrd range. Fires a volley of missiles at an enemy and its nearby allies, striking up to 10 targets as Physical damage.

Summon Skeleton

Summons 5 Skeletons to aid the caster in battle for 2 min.

Shoot

5-30 yrd range. Shoots at an enemy, inflicting Physical damage.
